1. Make sure you have a stable / dev chrome version above 18 . Now goto settings option from the browser window . 
2. Click on the extensions tab . Upload the extension Broplug . 
3. Click on the broplug webrtc icon apperaing in the chrome browser

5. The other peer also open the bropluf exntension as described in setps 1 to 3 . After this the peer enter the name of the existing room to join the webrtc session .
